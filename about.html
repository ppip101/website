About the Studio

Riftide is an independent game studio based in Finland consisting of an experienced team of developers: we're ambitious and driven, with strong backgrounds in game dev, marketing and storytelling.

Our mission is to craft unforgettable story-driven games: places to step into, experiences to feel, and stories that stay with you long after the game ends. We aim to become a trusted creator of narrative-rich games that bring fresh and unique voices to the game studio landscape.
